FAQ

How does it work?
When a user gets a receipt they will get a QR code that will take them to a review form. This code can only be used once and can't be changed. Its also tied back to their receipt so you know they actually purchased from you.

H ow much does it cost?
It's free to start and free to use for your existing customers. We charge $2 per referred booking and a $100/month for access to analytics to improve your business.

C an stores post fake positive reviews?
Our analytics will analyse all reviews and flag suspicious reviews from stores, eventually de-rating them. We'd love to tell you how it works but then it wouldn't be effective anymore.
